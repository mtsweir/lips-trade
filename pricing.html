Frequently asked questions

How are your LIPS data reports different from the Chart-Builder?

Our raw data reports are csv files (excel) which show detailed advertised pricing information for every product in a selected period. CSV files can be easily integrated with sales data and other excel based reports. The Chart-builder is customized software which allows you to instantly produce charts and identify pricing insights and advertising trends at the push of a button. Charts can be saved, and exported to power pt and excel. The charts will also automatically update with the latest data each time you log in.

We only require LIPS advertised pricing data & chart-builder for spirits. Is this possible?

Yes, we can set-up your subscription for spirits only, or for multiple alcohol categories (ie Beer, Wine, Cider, Spirits and/or RTD’s).

Can I subscribe to LIPS data & chart-builder for just a single sales channel?

Yes, we can set up a subscription for just single, or multiple sales channels (ie Grocery, Retail Chains and/or Independents)

As a South Island retailer only, can we subscribe to your LIPS data & chart-builder for just this region?

Yes, you can subscribe to our data reports and chart-builder for one, multiple or all regions. (ie Upper North Island (Taupo North), Lower North Island, South Island).
